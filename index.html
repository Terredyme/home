The Contech (Construction Technology) sector is rapidly transforming the construction industry by integrating new technologies, tools, and innovations. The global Contech market is expected to grow significantly, with projections estimating it will reach $2.1 trillion by 2030, expanding at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 8.7% from 2023 to 2030. This growth is driven by increasing demand for efficiency, sustainability, and safety in construction projects. Despite the rapid adoption of digital technologies in many sectors, the construction industry is still lagging. Only 15% of construction companies are fully digitized, while 60% still use paper-based processes for project management and documentation. Automation in construction, including robots for bricklaying, excavation, and surveying, is gaining traction. The construction robotics market is forecast to grow from $47 million in 2020 to $1.9 billion by 2027. The use of drones in construction is expected to save $100 billion annually by improving project monitoring, reducing rework, and minimizing delays. Autonomous construction vehicles are also on the rise, helping to reduce labor costs and improve safety.

A Vision for a Smart, Sustainable, and Safe Ecosystem. Building Space for Senses!

The Contech sector is increasingly focused on sustainable construction. Green building materials and methods, energy-efficient systems, latest technologies are growing in demand. The green construction market is estimated to be worth $364 billion by 2025, driven by stricter environmental regulations and a global push for reducing carbon footprints. Contech is also focused on improving the supply chain by integrating digital solutions that track materials, manage inventory, and optimize procurement. This helps mitigate risks of delays caused by material shortages or transportation issues. As cities become more urbanized, the demand for smarter, more sustainable infrastructure grows. The smart city market is projected to reach $1.7 trillion by 2026, with construction technologies playing a central role in creating energy-efficient buildings, traffic management systems, and urban planning solutions. These facts highlight how the Contech sector is at the forefront of innovation in the construction industry, addressing long-standing challenges while also embracing sustainability, efficiency, and cutting-edge technologies. The future of construction is increasingly shaped by digital solutions and smart technologies, which promise to enhance the entire lifecycle of construction projects—from planning and design to execution and maintenance.

Our long-term vision is to create connected & intelligent ecosystem in the Contech industry that spans all sectors & build space for senses.
